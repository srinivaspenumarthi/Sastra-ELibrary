About SASTRA Deemed University

SASTRA deemed university stands as a premier institution offering comprehensive education across multiple campuses. The Thanjavur campus and SRC campus provide world-class facilities for students pursuing various engineering disciplines. With SASTRA vision 2035, the university continues to expand its reach and impact in technical education.

Know SASTRA through its commitment to excellence in education, research, and placement opportunities. Our free campus environment fosters learning and innovation across all schools and departments.

Engineering Programs and Specializations

Mechanical Engineering

Our mechanical engineering department prepares students for diverse industrial opportunities. The curriculum includes theoretical foundations and practical applications essential for modern mechanical engineering careers. Students gain expertise in design, manufacturing, and thermal systems.

Electronics Engineering

Electronics engineering students at SASTRA develop skills in circuit design, signal processing, and embedded systems. The program emphasizes both analog and digital electronics, preparing graduates for the rapidly evolving technology sector and electronics manufacturing industries.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering education at SASTRA focuses on infrastructure development, structural design, and sustainable construction practices. Students learn modern construction techniques and project management skills essential for contemporary civil engineering projects.

Computing and Technology

The computing programs at SASTRA encompass various aspects of computer science and information technology. Students develop programming skills, learn system design, and gain expertise in emerging technologies that drive modern software development.

Admissions 2025 - Join SASTRA

Admissions 2025 applications are now open for various programmes to students across different disciplines. The application for 2025 includes opportunities in engineering, sciences, and specialized programs. Online registration 2025 makes the process convenient for prospective students.

SASTRA offers admissions across multiple schools including engineering, sciences, and other specialized departments. The university provides comprehensive support for admissions processes and helps students choose the right programs based on their interests and career goals.

First Year Programs

First year students at SASTRA receive comprehensive orientation and academic support. The first year curriculum is designed to build strong foundational knowledge across all engineering disciplines. Students are introduced to various specializations during their initial year to help them make informed choices.

Training Centers and Skill Development

Bajaj Engineering Skill Training

The Bajaj engineering skill training program provides hands-on experience with industry-standard equipment and processes. This specialized training enhances student capabilities and improves their employability in the competitive job market.

MHI Training Centre

Our MHI training centre offers advanced technical training programs designed to meet industry requirements. Students gain practical experience and develop skills that are directly applicable in professional engineering environments.

Skill Development Programme

The skill development programme at SASTRA focuses on enhancing both technical and soft skills. These programs are designed to prepare students for the challenges of modern engineering careers and help them become well-rounded professionals.

Faculty Corner and Academic Excellence

The faculty corner serves as a hub for academic interaction and knowledge sharing. Our distinguished faculty members bring extensive experience from both academia and industry, ensuring students receive world-class education and mentorship.

Faculty at SASTRA are committed to excellence in teaching and research. They actively engage with students through various academic activities and provide guidance for career development and placement preparation.

Graduate and Research Opportunities

SASTRA offers opportunities for graduate trainee engineer positions and junior research fellow roles. Graduate trainee engineer programs provide practical experience while diploma trainee engineer positions offer specialized training paths for technical careers.

Academic Calendar and Semester System

The commencement of classes for each semester follows a well-structured academic calendar. Students can track odd semester schedules and end semester exam results through the university's academic management system. Commencement of classes is announced well in advance to help students prepare.

The odd semester system at SASTRA ensures balanced academic load distribution throughout the year. Branch submission deadlines and academic milestones are clearly communicated to help students manage their academic progress effectively.

Specialized Programs and Schools

SASTRA campus hosts multiple schools offering diverse educational opportunities. The schools include engineering, sciences, management, and other specialized departments. Each school maintains its unique identity while contributing to the overall university ecosystem.

Education at SASTRA extends to specialized programs including optometry and law programmes. These diverse offerings make SASTRA a comprehensive educational institution catering to various academic interests and career aspirations.

Sciences and Research

The sciences department at SASTRA offers cutting-edge research opportunities and academic programs. Students in sciences develop strong analytical and research skills that prepare them for careers in research and development.
